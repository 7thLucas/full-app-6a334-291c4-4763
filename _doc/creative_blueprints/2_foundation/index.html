The Problem

Revenue lost, staff stretched, care fragmented.

  • Dental clinics lose 10–30% of scheduled revenue to no-shows — most preventable with a timely reminder.

  • Staff juggle separate scheduling, charting, and billing tools — creating errors and duplicated work across every patient interaction.

  • Treatment plans and invoices live apart from appointment records, slowing both care delivery and collections.

The Solution

One platform connecting every step of the patient journey.

  • Automated SMS & email reminders fire at configurable intervals — staff do nothing extra, and no-shows drop significantly.

  • Appointments, treatment plans, and billing live in a single unified patient profile — no switching, no duplication.

  • Invoices generate directly from completed appointments in under 30 seconds — accurate, instant, no re-entry required.
