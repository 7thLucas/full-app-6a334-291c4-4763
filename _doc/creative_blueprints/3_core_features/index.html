Core Features

Built around the people who use it.

Front Desk Clinical Staff Practice Manager

Appointment Calendar

Calendar-based booking, rescheduling, and cancellation with conflict detection and a daily overview panel for front desk staff.

Automated Reminders

SMS & email reminders fire automatically at configurable intervals — 48 h and 24 h before each appointment. No manual effort needed.

No-Show Tracking

Log missed appointments instantly. Track no-show patterns per patient and flag high-risk bookings for proactive follow-up before the next visit.

Patient Profile & Check-In

One-click check-in updates status and alerts clinical staff. Every patient's appointments, treatment plans, and billing history in one unified view.
