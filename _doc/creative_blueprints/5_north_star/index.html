What to count (verified operations)

Appointment confirmed & marked complete

Invoice generated from a completed appointment

Clicks, logins, and page views do not count.

Weekly Volume Formula

N dentists — adjust × appts / day — adjust × 5 days = WVO / week

Example: 2 dentists × 8 appts/day × 5 days = 80 verified ops / week
